Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish that is known for its delicious combination of flavors and textures. It typically includes rice noodles, shrimp or chicken, tofu, eggs, bean sprouts, peanuts, and a tangy sauce made from tamarind paste, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. The dish is often garnished with fresh herbs and lime wedges for added flavor. Pad Thai is a beloved street food in Thailand and has gained popularity worldwide for its unique taste and satisfying ingredients.

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp or chicken
  • Tofu
  • Eggs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Peanuts
  • Tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice
